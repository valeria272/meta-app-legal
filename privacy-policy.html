Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our application connected to Meta (Facebook/Instagram) platforms.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your Meta account to our application, we may access the following data depending on the permissions you grant:

  • Basic profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Pages and ad accounts you manage
  • Advertising campaign data and performance metrics
  • Audience insights and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Provide and operate the advertising management features of the app
  • Display your campaign performance and analytics
  • Generate reports and recommendations based on your ad data
  • Improve the functionality of the application

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Any data accessed through the Meta API is used in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers unless explicitly required for the core functionality of the application. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the Meta (Facebook) API. Your use of Meta platforms is governed by Meta's own Privacy Policy, available at https://www.facebook.com/policy.php.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7 below).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent and disconnect your Meta account at any time

7. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please visit our Data Deletion page or contact us directly. We will process your request within 30 days.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@yourdomain.com

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
