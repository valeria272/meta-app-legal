Data Deletion Request

Last updated: April 6, 2025

In accordance with Meta's Platform Terms and applicable privacy regulations (including GDPR and CCPA), you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data that our application has accessed or stored.

Meta Login Data Deletion: If you connected your account via Facebook Login, you can also remove our app's access directly from your Facebook settings. Instructions are provided below.

What Data We Delete

Upon receiving a valid deletion request, we will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information (name, email, user ID) stored in our system
  • Any cached advertising data associated with your account
  • Your authentication tokens and session data
  • Any preferences or settings saved under your account

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1 — Email Request

  1. Send an email to contact@yourdomain.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your full name and the email address associated with your account
  4. We will confirm receipt within 72 hours and complete the deletion within 30 days

Option 2 — Remove App from Facebook Settings

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find our application in the list of connected apps
  3. Click "Remove" to revoke access and delete associated data
  4. Confirm the removal when prompted

This immediately revokes our app's access to your Facebook/Instagram data.

Confirmation

After your data has been successfully deleted, you will receive a confirmation email. Please note that some data may be retained for a limited period if required by law or to resolve disputes.

Questions

If you have questions about the data deletion process or our privacy practices, please review our Privacy Policy or contact us at contact@yourdomain.com.
